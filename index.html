ABOUT

The person behind
the work.

I’ve lived in six countries and think globally by default, but for the past year, I’ve been in the GTA.

I’m someone who follows curiosity across disciplines that aren’t supposed to talk to each other.

Tech, policy, business, journalism - I refuse to pick one.

What ties them together is people: who gets heard, who gets left out, and how we close that distance.

I want to leave the door open behind me.

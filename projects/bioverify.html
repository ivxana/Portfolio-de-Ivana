BioVerify

Early Flu Detection for Schools

Overview

BioVerify is a low-cost biosensing system designed to detect influenza signals in school air and provide verified early warnings to administrators before outbreaks escalate.

The project explored how environmental sensing, embedded hardware, and AI verification systems could work together to support faster institutional response inside high-density school environments.

TYPE Biosensing System
FOCUS Biotech · AI · Public Health
STATUS Concept & Prototype

Why We Built It

What if schools could detect outbreaks before they become visible at scale?

Traditional flu surveillance systems are reactive. By the time outbreaks are confirmed through conventional reporting systems, transmission has often already spread across classrooms and communities.

BioVerify was built to explore how schools could move from reactive outbreak response to proactive environmental monitoring.

The Concept

Continuous air sampling. AI-verified alerts.

BioVerify continuously samples classroom air for influenza markers using a compact environmental sensing device. Signals are processed through onboard hardware, transmitted to a cloud system, cross-verified using AI logic, and translated into verified alerts for school administrators.

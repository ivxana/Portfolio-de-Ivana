03 Community · Mental Health · Youth Leadership

Service.

Small actions. Real impact.

Overview

I don’t just serve communities - I serve communities I’ve been part of. That’s the through-line.

A year of consistent community work - and ongoing: running mental health programming for 2,000+ students at my school, supporting global STEM education for young women, and contributing to civic engagement nonprofits.

FOCUS Community · Mental Health
SPAN 1 Year
STATUS Ongoing

Why It Mattered

Building tools is one form of impact. Showing up for people, week after week, is another.

I do both because they teach different things. Tech teaches me to build systems. Service teaches me to stay when things are slow, unglamorous, and still necessary.

Roles

Four communities. One thread.

Knights for Safe Spaces

Co-Executive

Running mental health programming for 2,000+ students at my school, with targeted support for international students.

The Pink Stairs

Reflection

Most of this work isn’t visible. Most of it doesn’t get an award.

But programming for 2,000 students, one event at a time, is how cultures actually change. I show up because these communities showed up for me first.

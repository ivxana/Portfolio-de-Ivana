The Pink Stairs

Outreach Executive  ·  April 2026 - Present

Overview

Supporting young women entering STEM - at scale.

The Pink Stairs is a global nonprofit dedicated to increasing the representation of young women in STEM fields. The organization reaches 8.4 million girls across 24 countries through programs, partnerships, and community-building efforts.

As Outreach Executive, I connect the organization with mission-aligned institutions, build partnerships, and expand the network of communities The Pink Stairs can reach.

  • Applying the same outreach skills I built through TKS to a much bigger mission

Why It Matters to Me

The same barriers that exist in tech - representation, access, being taken seriously before you’ve proven yourself - start way earlier than anyone acknowledges.

The Pink Stairs operates at that earlier layer. Getting more young women into STEM isn’t just a pipeline issue - it’s a question of who gets to define what the future looks like. I want to be part of answering that correctly.

01 Civic Tech · AI · Democracy

Tallied

Voting, Personalized.

Overview

AI-powered civic engagement platform that personalizes political information for first-time and young voters in Canada.

Built to help users understand how policies affect their own lives through personalized issue prioritization, policy comparison, and conversational AI.

PLATFORM tallied.ca ↗
STATUS Live

Why I Built It

What if democracy spoke your language?

I became frustrated trying to understand Canadian politics as a young voter and recent immigrant. Political information existed, but it felt fragmented, overly technical, difficult to navigate, and disconnected from everyday life.

Tallied was built around a simple question: what if political information could meet people where they actually are?

Reflection

Civic disengagement is often less about apathy and more about accessibility.

Tallied became an exploration of how technology, when intentionally designed, can help make democratic participation feel more understandable, personal, and relevant to younger generations.

Democracy was designed before the internet. Tallied was a small attempt at bridging that gap.

01 Civic Tech

Tallied

A personalized voting guide that helps citizens make informed decisions at the ballot box.

Overview

Tallied is a civic technology product designed to bridge the gap between complex electoral information and everyday voters. The platform curates personalized voting guides based on user values, local context, and candidate positions.

The project sits at the intersection of technology, policy, and public participation — making democratic processes more accessible and less overwhelming for first-time and experienced voters alike.

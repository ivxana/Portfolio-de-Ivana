Ferra Labs

Drill Target Intelligence for Stimulated Geologic Hydrogen

Overview

Ferra Labs is an AI exploration platform focused on improving drill target intelligence for stimulated geologic hydrogen systems.

The platform explores how geospatial intelligence, subsurface data, and machine learning can help transform geologic hydrogen targeting from geological guesswork into confidence-scored exploration decisions.

The Problem

Stimulated geologic hydrogen faces a major targeting challenge.

Operators need higher-confidence drill targets to justify pilot drilling and secure investment, but existing geological datasets are often too coarse for commercially viable exploration planning.

Ferra Labs explored how AI-assisted targeting systems could help bridge that gap.

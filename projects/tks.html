02 Education · Innovation

TKS

Accelerating the world's most ambitious young people through immersive, high-impact programming.

Overview

The Knowledge Society (TKS) is a global program that trains young people to think like the world's most impactful innovators. Through a rigorous curriculum centered on emerging technologies, systems thinking, and human impact, TKS accelerates students to work on the world's hardest problems.

This work explores what it means to develop the next generation of leaders equipped not just with technical skills, but with the conviction and capacity to act on them.

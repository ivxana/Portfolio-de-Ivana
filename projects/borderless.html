Borderless

Journalism Intern  ·  2026 – Present

The Org

Championing international students — their stories, their futures.

Borderless was founded by a former low-income international student who knew firsthand how isolating and opaque the process of studying abroad can be. The platform spreads the stories of international students and provides trustworthy college-admissions counseling and AI tools — built for people who don’t have a guidance counselor who’s done this before.

It reaches over 200,000 readers, 100K+ monthly social views, and nearly 50,000 Instagram followers. The audience is global, and so is the impact.

My Role

Journalism Intern.

I interview international students about their journeys and write their stories. The process involves research, outreach, sourcing, and writing — and a lot of listening.

These articles have real impact. Students who don’t see themselves represented in college admissions, who dream of leaving their countries — they read these. I used to read them myself, before I came to Canada. Many of the students I now interview have read them too.

It’s full circle. And I love it.
